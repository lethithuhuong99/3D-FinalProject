STARS WAR

×
After the end of the civil war in the galaxy, your spaceship has been separated from your legion. With the remaining food and fuel on board you need to quickly cross 100 planets to bring everyone on board back to their families. On the way you need to avoid colliding with planets to ensure the safety of the ship. You can speed up for faster return, as well as slow down for time to rest.

How to play?

×

1. Press any key to start game

2. Press the 's'/'<' key to move left

3. Press the 'd'/'>' key to move left

4. Pick up flash box to speed up

5. Pick up slow box to speed down
